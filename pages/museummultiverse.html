What is Museum Multiverse

Museum Multiverse is a virtual reality puzzle platformer. This 3rd person experience starts with a child waking up in an abandoned museum. He must travel into the world of the art pieces around him, in order to find a way out of this cursed museum. The museum is abandoned and sealed off. It is a manifestation of society’s diminished view of minority artists, and history’s amnesia toward the contributions of people of color.
The player will visit worlds based on the work of underrepresented minority artists, like Horace Pippin, Kara Walker, William H Johnson and Frida Kahlo. In this way, the experience will rectify history, and give voice to the previously unheard, and to those we have collectively forgotten, by bringing their worlds to life in a new context.

Project Vision

Museum Multiverse brings fun gameplay to VR

Museum Multiverse will rectify the problem of passive VR by introducing inventive gameplay, mixing 3D platforming with VR mechanics utilizing the new Gear VR controller. Throughout the game the player will travel into works of art created by underrepresented minority artists in order to address the problem of underrepresentation in VR and gaming in general. Museum Multiverse will use mechanics-as-metaphor, utilizing the artists’ works within the gameplay. These mechanics-as-metaphor will serve to tell the story of a child finding their way through a forgotten relic in time as they learn and grow within the world.
